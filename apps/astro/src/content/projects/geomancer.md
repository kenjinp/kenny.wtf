# Geomancer

Geomancer is a “Google Earth for fantasy worlds” — a tool for exploring an invented world the way you’d explore Earth.

This is where I’m experimenting with world-scale rendering, navigation/UX, and the workflow for authoring and viewing fictional geospatial data.
